## 3Ô∏è‚É£ WGI Compliance Architecture

1. **KYC Engine**

   * ID verification (passport, national ID)
   * Proof of address
   * Biometric verification (optional)
   * Sanction & PEP screening

2. **AML & Transaction Monitoring**

   * Real-time transaction monitoring
   * Alerts for suspicious activity
   * Automated reporting to regulators

3. **Regulatory Reporting**

   * Daily, monthly, and quarterly reports
   * FX conversion summaries
   * Settlement reconciliations
   * Audit-ready transaction logs

4. **Data Protection & Privacy**

   * Encryption at rest & in transit (AES-256, TLS 1.3)
   * Regional storage compliance (local servers or approved cloud zones)
   * GDPR and African Data Protection regulations compliance

5. **Bank & Liquidity Partner Integration**

   * Contracts defining regulatory responsibilities
   * Automated reconciliation with Wekeza Bank and partner banks
   * Multi-bank compliance logging
