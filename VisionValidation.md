## 1. Executive Summary

Wekeza Global Infrastructure (WGI) is a bank-agnostic cross-border financial infrastructure platform designed to enable African individuals and businesses to receive, manage, convert, and deploy global currencies seamlessly.

Unlike traditional banks or standalone fintech wallets, WGI integrates with multiple banks, card networks, and liquidity providers to create a unified multi-currency wallet and settlement engine.

The platform will:

* Provide global collection accounts (USD, EUR, GBP)
* Enable instant FX conversion
* Allow withdrawals to any Kenyan bank
* Offer virtual USD cards
* Support multi-bank integrations (including Wekeza Bank)
* Build toward a credit intelligence engine powered by transaction behavior

WGI positions itself as infrastructure, not just a wallet ‚Äî enabling freelancers, SMEs, exporters, and fintech startups to plug into global commerce rails efficiently.
